Our Story

Born from a Real Problem

It started when we noticed coaching centers and teachers spending countless hours typing question papers, study notes, and exam materials by hand. The process was slow, error-prone, and took time away from what they do best — teaching.

Founded in 2024 in Prachin Mayapur, MM Graphics was created to solve this exact problem. We built a service that turns handwritten pages into perfectly formatted, print-ready digital documents — delivered fast and at prices that make sense.

What began as a local service quickly grew through word of mouth. Today, we serve 50+ coaching centers and educational institutions, having completed over 500 projects and typed more than 10,000 pages. Our commitment to accuracy, speed, and fair pricing keeps clients coming back.
